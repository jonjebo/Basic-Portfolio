Good morning, afternoon, or night to you! My name is Jonathan Dangelo and I am currently writing my first "About Me" page.
I currently live in sunny SoCal, but am originally from a small farming town in Ohio where I was born and raised. Once I graduated
high school I moved to Colorado where I spent a few years in Vail snowboarding and enjoying life! When I was there I had some of
best experiences of my life and made some of the best friends I've ever had. Vail is a place that gets visitors from all over the
world and every year I made new friends from other countries. I do not regret one day that I spent there.

Then my world changed and I decided that I wanted to gain my Bachelors from CSU in Fort Collins, Colorado. I then spent the next
five years there going to school and getting used to the change of pace from Vail. I made many more friends and had many nights
nights out with friends over beers. The 5 years in Fort Collins were some of the best I've had as well because I loved the town
town and the people I was surrounded by. Colorado has become much more fast paced since I have left a couple years ago. Around
the time I moved away many people started moving to Colorado and have changed the environment. It is much larger now and there
are many more people.

I have traveled over half of this beautiful country and a few countries across the world and my traveling has not finshed yet. I
am soon to have a baby boy and hope that we will be able to continue my traveling together so that both of us can experince the
world that we live in together. I still haven't found the state that I want to call home, but I feel I am getting closer. It makes
me sad to leave my friends and move to new places, but it also allows me to gain new friends that I would never have me otherwise.

Thank You for reading a little bit about me and where I come from! Best of wishes on your future endevors!
